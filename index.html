Gameplay:

    > Input a username and hit Enter

    > Click within the game window to activate controls

    > Use arrow keys and / or your cursor to move

    > Press 'ESC' / Escape to leave cursor control

    > Press 'F' to change player sprite

    > Increase your score by surviving and eating cookies

    > Start with 3 hitpoints, every 10 cookies eaten = 1 hitpoint

    > DODGE THE VEGETABLES!
